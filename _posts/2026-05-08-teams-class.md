Today I setup a MS Team for my class, ROP631.
The [Guide](https://www.usherbrooke.ca/documentation-ena/#$1:Integrer_Teams-Moodle_Guide_demarrage) explains that you have to

* go in Moodle
* click the `<` symbol in the upper right
* add Microsoft block (+ Ajouter un bloc)
* login
* click Paramètres d'intégration Teams
* create new Team
* wait until it is created
